Miki's island escape!

Welcome to Miki's island escape, a game developed by Doodle Games in pure JavaScript. Feel free to register a profile and try out the game. Below you can read about the characters and the quest.

Sign up

Player

You play as Miki, he is a survior of the dangerous sea. He nearly drowned but he luckily managed to drift up to shore on an undiscovered island.

Enemies

You will encounter enemies along the way, the island is infested with dangerous zombies. Beware, if you walk into them they will bite and you will lose life!

Objective

Your main goal is the pick up enough banans so that the gorilla king will fly you home, if you survive both levels he will wait for you at the end exit point.

Collect bananas

The bananas is your collectables, you will need 10 bananas for the king to have an interest in flying you home. If you try to buy is friendship with less than 10 bananas he won't help you at all and you lose the game.
